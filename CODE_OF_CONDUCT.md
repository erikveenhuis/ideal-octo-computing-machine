# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want a harassment-free experience for everyone who participates in this
project, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Accept good-faith criticism and feedback
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, trolling, and publishing
others’ private information are not tolerated.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, please contact the
maintainers via a **GitHub issue** (you may ask for a private follow-up in
the issue text). Maintainers will review reports and respond as they are able.

This code of conduct is adapted from common open-source community guidelines.
